This website is designed for small business owners, such as plumbers, to showcase their services, products, and proposals in a professional and accessible way.

Proposal

This website proposal aims to create an engaging, professional, and user-friendly online presence for small business owners. By providing clear navigation, informative content, and a visually appealing design, the website will help attract new clients and retain existing ones.

The design uses a combination of white, blue, black, red, light blue, orange, and grey colors to balance professionalism with creativity. Images of past projects, service galleries, and product listings will be included to showcase expertise and build trust.
